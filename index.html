Spiderman

Spider-Man is one of the most iconic superheroes, loved by audiences worldwide. The movie follows Peter Parker, a high school student who gains superhuman abilities after being bitten by a radioactive spider.

Black spider-man

The black-suited Spider-Man, first introduced during the Secret Wars storyline, represents Peter Parker's darker side after bonding with an alien symbiote that enhances his abilities but corrupts his behavior.
